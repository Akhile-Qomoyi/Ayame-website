About AYAME

AYAME (from Japanese: Iris; Sprout; Love; Blessing) is a gender-neutral name that symbolizes resilience and balance. In isiXhosa, it also means “we lean on God” — a message of hope for the people. AYAME is a stealthy mobile app designed to protect vulnerable individuals, especially women, from gender-based violence in South Africa. Built to work even without mobile data, it offers silent triggers, hidden features, and real-time safety alerts.

Key Features

Stealth Mode

Disguised as a calculator app with a secret PIN to access the real settings and emergency functions.

Silent Panic Triggers

Activate emergency mode by pressing the power button 3 times or long-pressing the volume button, without alerting perpetrators.

Live GPS + Recording

Instantly send your precise location and silent photo/audio evidence to authorities and secure cloud storage.

Offline & Bundle Support

Access the app even without data or using special low-cost network bundles, similar to Facebook FreeMode or WhatsApp bundles.
